Our Mission

Phoenix Solutions sees the potential in our clients and sets out to make the most of what they have, rather than to fix what they do not. Phoenix Solutions is uplifting, right down to its name. It is a name that symbolizes hope, rebirth, redemption and all things positive. There is nothing contrived or over the top about our optimism.

Our Story

On September 10, 2018, a daring venture was started at Pelican Bay State Prison to teach prisoners computer programming. It focused on helping prisoners build relevant skills in technology and other areas so that they can more easily transition to productive employment once they are out of prison. We at Phoenix Solutions seek to rise from our circumstances and create innovative products for our customers.
