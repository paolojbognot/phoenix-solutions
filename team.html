Our Team

Phoenix Solutions is run by Paolo Bognot. He is one of the original graduates of The Last Mile's Code.7370 "We're In Beta" Class at Pelican Bay State Prison.

He speaks "computer" and uses his uncanny skill to make computers do what he tells them.

Paolo Bognot currently works as the Teacher's Assistant at Pelican Bay State Prison's Code.7370 cohort, where he helps and supports the coding students in their programming endeavors. You can easily spot him with his signature dark frame glasses and hairdo around the classroom translating the code to the computers for the students.
